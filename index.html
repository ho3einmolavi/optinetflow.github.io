Frequently asked questions

Answers to most common questions.

How does multi-brand deployment work?

Our platform allows you to deploy your VPN service across multiple brands and domains. Each brand can have its own customized user interface and configuration, providing a tailored experience for different audiences.

Can I manage different brands from a single dashboard?

Yes, you can manage all your brands from a single dashboard. This allows you to streamline operations, monitor performance, and handle configurations for multiple brands efficiently.

How do I set up custom Telegram bots for each brand?

Each brand can have its own dedicated Telegram bot. You can customize these bots to handle user interactions, provide support, and automate tasks. Setup instructions are provided in the dashboard, or you can contact our support team for assistance.

Can I integrate Telegram bots with other tools or services?

Yes, our Telegram bots can be integrated with various tools and services, including CRM systems, ticketing systems, and more. Custom integrations can be arranged based on your specific needs.

How many Telegram bots can I have for each brand?

Each brand is allocated one dedicated Telegram bot. If you need additional bots or have specific requirements, please reach out to our support team to discuss custom solutions.

Can I customize the responses and features of my Telegram bots?

Yes, you can customize the responses and features of your Telegram bots through our dashboard. This includes setting up automated replies, commands, and interactions tailored to your brand’s needs.
